About Movie Recommender

Movie Recommender is a website that helps you discover new movies based on your preferences. Simply enter the genre you're in the mood for and the movies you've already watched, and we'll recommend a list of movies tailored just for you!

Our recommendation system uses data from The Movie Database (TMDb) to provide accurate and up-to-date suggestions. Whether you're into action, comedy, drama, or something else, we've got you covered.
